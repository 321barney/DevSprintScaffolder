üí∞ Complete Transaction Flow
1

Tourist Posts Job

System uses AI to generate dynamic price band (e.g., 150-250 MAD) based on category, city, time, and historical data

2

Provider Submits Offer

System checks: Do they have free offers remaining? If yes, allow (decrement counter). If no, require subscription upgrade.

3

AI Scores Offer

Claude evaluates offer quality vs. competition, provider reputation, and fair pricing. Returns score (0-1) with reasoning.

4

Tourist Accepts Offer

Offer price: 200 MAD ‚Üí Commission (15%): 30 MAD ‚Üí Provider gets: 170 MAD. Platform fee record created instantly.

5

Commission Recorded

Database entries: platform_fees table (30 MAD), provider_earnings table (170 MAD - available after 7 days)

6

Provider Runs Out of Free Offers

After 4th offer, system blocks submission and prompts: "Subscribe to Basic (299 MAD/month) or Pro (799 MAD/month)"

7

Subscription Payment

Provider clicks "Upgrade" ‚Üí Redirected to CMI/PayZone ‚Üí Pays 299 MAD ‚Üí PSP webhook ‚Üí Subscription activated

8

Revenue Recognition

Platform earns: Commission (30 MAD) + Subscription (299 MAD) = 329 MAD from this provider this month
